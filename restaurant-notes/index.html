      Share from Instagram

      Do this once. Then Instagram → Share → Resto Notes.

      Part 1 — Turn on Share Sheet

      1. Open the Shortcuts app → tap + (top right).
      2. Tap ⓘ (info) → turn on Show in Share Sheet → tap Done (blue checkmark).
      3. Back in the shortcut editor, look at the very top — a new step appeared: Receive … from Share Sheet.
      4. Tap the blue word Any (or Anything) in that step → tap Clear → turn on URLs only → Done.

      No “Share Sheet Types” menu? That’s normal on newer iPhones — use the Receive step at the top instead.

      Part 2 — Add 4 actions

      Tap Add Action below the Receive step. Search the word in bold.

      1. encode → URL Encode → tap Input → choose Shortcut Input
      2. Text → tap inside the box → copy & paste this line:
      https://mathoose.github.io/cursor-apps/restaurant-notes/?quickAdd=1&instagram=
      1. combine → Combine Text → first part: Text (step 3) · second part: URL Encoded Text (step 2)
      2. open → Open URLs

      Part 3 — Name & save

      1. Tap the shortcut name at the top → rename Resto Notes → Done.

      How to use it

      Instagram post → Share → scroll the bottom row → tap Resto Notes. This app opens with the link filled in.

      How to use

      Save a spot from Instagram

      1. In Instagram, tap Share on the post → Copy link.
      2. Open Resto Notes (this app) → tap + Add.
      3. Tap Paste link next to Instagram. Add the name and anything else.
      4. Tap Done. It saves automatically.

      Move into Philly Dates

      1. Here: tap Copy all for import.
      2. Open Philly Dates → Settings (⚙️).
      3. Scroll to Import from text → paste → Import pasted text.

      Share from Instagram in one tap
